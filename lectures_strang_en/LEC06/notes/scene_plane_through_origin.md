# Scene: Plane Through the Origin

## Title
Linear combinations generate a plane

---

## Objective

Visualize how two vectors generate a plane using linear combinations.

---

## Concept

P = { c v + d w | c,d ∈ R }  
P = span{v, w}

---

## Key Idea

- Forms a plane  
- Passes through origin  
- Therefore: subspace  

---

## Sequence

1. Title  
2. Axes  
3. Origin  
4. Vector v  
5. Vector w  
6. c v + d w  
7. Plane appears  
8. Label span  
9. Moving point  
10. Emphasis origin  
11. Conclusion  

---
